Emergency Supply Checklist

Preparing an emergency kit is important because it ensures you're ready to handle unexpected situations such as natural disasters, power outages, or accidents. Having essential supplies on hand can help you stay safe, manage basic needs, and maintain some level of comfort and security until help arrives or conditions improve. It can aid in communication and update you of necessary news.
